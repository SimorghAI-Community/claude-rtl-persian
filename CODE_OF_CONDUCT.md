# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to creating a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive community for everyone interested in improving Persian language accessibility in AI platforms.

## Expected Behavior

Community members should:

- Be respectful and constructive
- Welcome different perspectives
- Provide helpful feedback
- Focus on improving the project

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following are not acceptable:

- Harassment or discrimination
- Personal attacks
- Trolling or disruptive behavior
- Sharing harmful or inappropriate content

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please open a private communication with the maintainers.

Thank you for helping make this community better.
